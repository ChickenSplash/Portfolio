About Me

Where it Started

My programming journey began with a love for creativity and problem-solving through Minecraft datapacks. What started as tinkering grew into building packs for others, sparking my interest in how things work. This led me to explore 3D modeling with Blender, creating items for the Steam Workshop and avatars for VRChat.

What's Next

What drives me is the belief that with the right knowledge, I can build anything on a computer. That mindset keeps me eager to learn and grow across multiple domains; from front-end and back-end web development to cybersecurity and AI. My goal is to continually expand my skillset and carve out a versatile, meaningful role in the world of software development.

Netmatters Homepage

X

This project recreates Netmatters homepage using HTML, CSS, SASS, javaScript and PHP. The projects makes use of javaScript to add dynamic animations on the page such as the pop out sidemenu. This project also uses PHP to make us of include partials for things such as the header, footer and nav. I have also implemented a dynamic news section where content is pulled from a MySQL database and rendered using PHP, allowing for easy content management and updates.
